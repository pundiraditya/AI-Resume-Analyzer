AI Resume Analyzer

An AI-powered Resume Analyzer that helps job seekers optimize their resumes by analyzing content, calculating ATS compatibility scores, extracting key skills, identifying missing keywords, and providing personalized recommendations to improve interview chances.

📖 Overview

AI Resume Analyzer leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning techniques to evaluate resumes and provide actionable insights. The platform helps candidates understand how well their resumes align with ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) and industry requirements.
